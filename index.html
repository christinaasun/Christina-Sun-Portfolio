About me!

Hi! My name is Christina Sun. I am a current student at The College of New Jersey, and I am majoring in Graphic Design along with completing a minor in Marketing. I grew up in Ho-Ho-Kus, New Jersey, with four siblings, a dog, and our parents. I have always had a love for all things that allow me to express myself creatively. I have been dancing ever since I was a little girl, and I have always been creating new art over the years as well. I have grown a passion for the fact that there is no "right" answer in the design field, and people within the industry appreciate and learn from each others' works and creative ideas.
